Yuxin Guo

I'm currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Wei Zou.

Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree from Wuhan University in 2021, where I was supervised by Prof. Cien Fan.

My research interests lie in audio-visual multimodal learning. Currently, I focus on audio-video large language models, long-audio generation and unified multimodal understanding and generation, as well as video understanding.
